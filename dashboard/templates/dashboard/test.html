Goals and benefits:

By utilizing automation in agriculture, several improvements are made to the sector while also helping farmers in time and money benefits.There are so many benefits like:
  • 1. The robots can operate with tighter tolerances (so that every round is at full field capacity), they offer less mistakes and at greater speeds, and the machines can properly perceive the disease. They also do not get ill, tired, or require breaks.
  • 2. Robots may protect human employees from the risks associated with handling chemicals by hand and using a high-pressure spraying system. They can also cut the amount of pesticides used on farms by up to 80%.
  • 3. The robots can be used in various fields in agriculture.
  • 4. Trees, rocks, ponds, and other obstacles are simply navigated by the robots.
  • 5. In the future, the robots can do more or other tasks.
